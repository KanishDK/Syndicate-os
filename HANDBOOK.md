## 1. Core Systems

### 1.1 Economy (Clean vs Dirty Money)

**The Dual Currency System**:
- **Dirty Money** (Sorte Penge): Earned from illegal sales.
  - Generates heat when accumulated.
  - Cannot be used for official upgrades/staff.
- **Clean Money** (Rene Penge): Laundered profit or territory income.
  - Safe to use for all purchases.
  - Does not generate heat.

### 1.2 Production System

**12 Product Tiers**:
From Hash (Tier 1) to Fentanyl (Tier 4). Higher tiers offer massive revenue but generate significant Heat and require expensive base materials.
