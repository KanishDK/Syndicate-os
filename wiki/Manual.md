## 5. Heat & Police System

### Heat Mechanics
- **Generation**: Selling drugs generating heat based on tier. Territories generate passive heat.
- **Decay**: Heat drops slowly over time (Base: -6/min).
- **Reduction**: Hire Lawyers, use "Bribe Police" (Emergency), or buy luxury upgrades.

### Heat Thresholds ‚ö†Ô∏è
- **70%**: Yellow Warning. Police are watching.
- **90%**: Red Critical Alert. Raid imminent.
- **100%**: High Raid probability.

### Raids
- **Frequency**: ~17 minutes average at max heat.
- **Tiers**: Higher heat = tougher raids.
- **Consequence**: Loss of cash (10-25%) and product (20-50%).
- **Defense**: Guards, Cameras, and Bunkers help repel raids.
- **Hardcore Mode**: Raid loss = **PERMANENT GAME OVER**.

### Territories

- **Income**: Generates clean cash passively.

- **Strength Meter**: Watch the amber bar in the Rivals tab to know when to strike.

## 7. Money Laundering

### Methods
1. **Manual (Vaskeriet)**: Click to wash money (10% rate, 30% fee).
2. **Passive (Accountant)**: Hired staff launder automatically (+5% rate).
3. **Crypto**: Buy low, sell high to clean money via market gains.
4. **Bank**: Deposit clean cash to earn 0.5% interest every 5 minutes.
